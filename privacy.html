Privacy Policy

SilvaCruise — The Ultimate Forester's Tool

Summary

SilvaCruise does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All cruise data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it unless you choose to export it yourself.

Data Storage

All data you enter into SilvaCruise — including cruise configurations, plot data, tree measurements, and reports — is stored exclusively on your device in a local database. We have no servers, no cloud storage, and no way to access your data.

Data Collection

SilvaCruise does not collect any of the following: personal information (name, email, phone number, address), location data or GPS coordinates, usage analytics or telemetry, device identifiers or advertising IDs, crash reports or diagnostic data, cookies or tracking information.

Third-Party Services

SilvaCruise does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app contains no ads and does not sell or share any data with third parties.

Data Export

When you choose to export cruise data (as CSV or PDF), the exported files are handled by your device's native sharing system. SilvaCruise does not retain copies of exported files or have any knowledge of where you send them.

Children's Privacy

SilvaCruise is a professional forestry tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Since we collect no data, significant changes are unlikely.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to: silvacruiseapp@gmail.com
